What about cities?

Urban growth means less and less rural land left. As the populations grows more trash is produce which has the greatest potential to harm the environment, wildlife and humans.However, dense concentrations of tall buildings could help make urban centers greener and more energy efficient than sprawling suburbs.

Once natural resources start getting used up at faster rates, the advent of more technological advances leads to environmental friendly developements using green resources.
